Introduction
1.1
Content of this Manual
This manual (Part 2 of 2) is the specific description of 
SCANLAB’s varioSCANde II 20i FT dynamic focusing 
unit and covers the scope of delivery, as well as speci-
fications of the delivered system.
SCANLAB reserves the right to update this operating 
manual at any time and without notification. The 
information in this manual supersedes the content of 
previous manuals.
1.2
Use and Distribution of this 
Manual
This manual is part of the product. Read it carefully 
before proceeding to plan, install and operate the 
scan system. If there are any questions regarding the 
contents of this manual, please contact SCANLAB 
(see chapter 1.4).
SCANLAB typically supplies this manual in electronic 
form (as a PDF file).
Provide access to this manual in suitable form to 
all persons involved in planning, development, 
installation, commissioning and maintenance of 
the scan system.
Maintain accessibility to this manual even after 
planning.
This manual should accompany the scan system if 
ownership changes hands.
1.3
Additional Manuals
• Part 1 – General Description:
The general description provides a product over-
view, a functional description of the 
varioSCANde II 20i FT, safety guidelines, the 
description of installation and start-up as well as 
maintenance.
• If the varioSCAN II is to be used in conjunction 
with an XY scan system, then also read the docu-
mentation of the XY scan system.
• For a specific 3-axis configuration consisting of 
the varioSCAN II, an XY scan system and possibly 
an objective in conjunction with a correction file, 
SCANLAB will provide a data sheet listing that 
configuration’s optical properties.
• Furthermore, you will receive the Application 
Note “Calibrating a 3-Axis Laser Scan System” 
from SCANLAB.
